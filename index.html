About Me

My name is Benjamin Netanyahu, born at 1949 in Tel-Aviv, israel.

I am the second of three childerns, spent most of my live in jerusalem.

Moved with my family to Philadelphia, US for 6 years and returned to israel at 1967 to serve at the IDF.

Served as an officer at Sayeret Matkal and released from duty at 1972 as Captein .

During my army service, i have taken a part in many actions and wars which accour at that time, served as reserve soldier at the YOM KIPUR war .

Studied and graduated B.D. from MIT and also gradueted from Harverd.

After graduation, returned back to israel, and been a part of the israeli parlamnet in many ministerial roles such as:

  • minister of science and technology.
  • minister of housing and construction.
  • minister of foreign affairs.
  • minister of economic.
  • minister of health.
  • minister of communication.
  • Leader of the opposition.
  • Prime minister for 12 years 2009-2021.

Married to Sara and have 2 childern, Yair and Avner. currently living at Caesarea Maritima.

Differences between Web 1.0, web 2.0 and Web 3.0

  • Web 1.0 is read only - the user is only able to see the content of the page. Web 2.0 is read-write so the is interaction between the user and the website, Web 3.0 is read-write-execute.
  • Web 1.0 is using simple HTML and graphics based flash. Web 2.0 is a 2 way web page which uses wikis,videos, podcasts, shading and 2D poratls. Web 3.0 is using 3D portals, avtar representation, Interoperable profits, Multi-user Integrated games, education and business, all media flows in andout of virtual web worlds
  • Web 1.0 was all about static content, one way publishing of content without any real interaction between readers or publishers or each other.Web 2.0 is more about 2 way communication through social networking, blogging, wikis,tagging, user generated content and video. Web 3.0 is curiusly undefined. AI and the web learning what you want and delivering you a Personalized web experience
